The daily reality of memory loss

These aren't just medical symptoms. They're moments of lost connection.

Not recognizing loved ones

A daughter walks in and her father doesn't know her name. It happens every day to millions of families.

Withdrawing from conversation

The fear of forgetting a word or name leads people to stop talking altogether. Silence replaces connection.

Losing independence too soon

People become dependent on full-time care far earlier than their physical health requires.

Cue AI gives that recognition back — quietly, instantly, and with dignity.

How it works

Three simple steps, working quietly in the background.

Recognize

Identifies the people around you using cameras and microphones in everyday wearables.

Remember

Knows their names, how you know them, and meaningful context from your shared history.

Remind

Delivers a discreet prompt the moment you need it — on glasses, earbuds, or your watch.

Who it's for

Memory Support

Vision Assistance

For people who are blind or have low vision and want to know who's nearby.

Family & Caregivers

For loved ones who want to support independence and preserve dignity.
