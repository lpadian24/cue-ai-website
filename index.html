The Challenge

Forgetting a name isn't just
forgetting a word. It's losing a connection.

For millions living with Alzheimer's and dementia, every conversation carries the anxiety of not recognizing the person in front of them. The embarrassment leads to withdrawal. Withdrawal accelerates decline.

Social Isolation

People withdraw from gatherings and conversations because forgetting names and words becomes too painful. Silence replaces connection.

Lost Independence

Without reliable recall, individuals depend on full-time caregivers far earlier than their physical condition requires, losing autonomy and dignity.

Caregiver Burnout

13 million family caregivers provide $500 billion in unpaid labor annually. The emotional, physical, and financial toll is immense and largely unaddressed.

Visual Impairment

12 million Americans with vision loss face the daily challenge of not knowing who they're speaking with. No existing tool offers real-time person identification.

How Cue AI Works

Intelligent assistance that
feels invisible.

Cue AI runs quietly on the devices you already wear. When someone approaches, you'll know exactly who they are before a word is spoken.

01

Recognize

Your smart glasses or AirPods camera detects a face in your field of view and instantly matches it against your personal knowledge graph using on-device AI.

02

Identify

If someone speaks before you see them, voiceprint matching identifies them by voice alone. Combined face + voice matching gives the highest confidence results.

03

Prompt

Within seconds, a discreet prompt tells you: "That's your daughter, Sarah. She visited last Tuesday." Delivered through your glasses display, earpiece, or watch.

Core Capabilities

Technology that understands
what you need, when you need it.

Flagship Innovation

Dual-Mode Identification

Face and voice recognition work together so you're supported in every situation—face-to-face, in dark rooms, on phone calls, or when someone speaks from behind you. No other product offers this.

Phone-to-Person Voiceprint Linking

Cue AI passively learns voices from your phone calls, linking them to contacts. When that person speaks to you in person, they're instantly recognized.

Hesitation Detection

AI trained on dementia-specific speech patterns detects word-finding difficulty in real time and proactively delivers the word before the moment passes.

Personal Knowledge Graph

A continuously learning map of your world—people, relationships, places, routines, and vocabulary—enriched by every interaction.

Visual Impairment Support

Identifies people by face and voice for blind and low-vision users, delivering names and context entirely through audio—no visual input required.

Wandering Prevention

GPS geofencing through Apple Watch and Samsung Galaxy Watch alerts caregivers instantly when a user leaves a designated safe area.

Cognitive Trend Monitoring

Tracks speech patterns over time, giving clinicians objective digital biomarkers to supplement traditional cognitive assessments.

Caregiver Dashboard

Family members and clinicians can manage the knowledge graph, view identification logs, monitor cognitive trends, and configure the system remotely.

Privacy by Design

Your memories stay
yours. Period.

Privacy isn't a feature we added. It's the architectural foundation everything is built on. Every piece of sensitive data—your face, your voice, your relationships—is processed on your own device and never leaves it.

On-Device Processing

All facial recognition, voiceprint matching, and speech analysis run locally on your phone's neural processing unit.

Zero Raw Data Transmission

No audio, video, or biometric data ever leaves your device. Only anonymized metadata is transmitted with your explicit consent.

Secure Enclave Storage

Voiceprints are stored in your device's hardware-level secure enclave and are never transmitted to any server.

HIPAA Compliant

Designed from the ground up for healthcare-grade data protection. BAAs maintained with all cloud partners.
