Why Cue AI

Forgetting a name isn't just
forgetting a word. It's losing a connection.

Millions of people live with the daily anxiety of not recognizing the person standing in front of them. Cue AI exists to give that recognition back.

Memory Support

For individuals with Alzheimer's, dementia, or age-related memory difficulties who want to stay connected to the people in their lives.

Vision Assistance

For people who are blind or have low vision, providing real-time information about who they're speaking with and the world around them.

Caregiver Peace of Mind

For families and caregivers who want to support their loved ones while preserving their independence and dignity.

What It Does

Intelligent assistance that
feels invisible.

Cue AI runs quietly in the background. When someone approaches, you'll know exactly who they are.

01

Recognize

Cue AI identifies the people around you using the cameras and microphones in your everyday devices.

02

Remember

It remembers names, relationships, and meaningful context about the people you care about most.

03

Remind

A discreet prompt tells you who they are, how you know them, and the last time you spoke — right when you need it.
