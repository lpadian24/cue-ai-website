How it works

Three simple steps, working quietly in the background.

Recognize

Identifies the people around you using cameras and microphones in everyday wearables.

Knows their names, how you know them, and meaningful context from your shared history.

Remind

Delivers a discreet prompt the moment you need it — on glasses, earbuds, or your watch.
